About

MovieJazz will help you find the lowest prices on movies, at a range of different theatres

This application was developed in 2 weeks, collating data from two separate providers, which exposed a RESTful API.

This application was required to run smoothly, without too much delay, even when the providers fell over. With some caching magic, we achieved a great middle ground, showing live data when possible, and resorting to cache when the providers fail.
